Why Re-Boot ?

Reasons to be cheerful...

Re-Boot is an online exchange that encourages customers to register, donate unwanted rugby boots and purchase replacement boots at a fraction of normal retail prices.

A 'one stop shop' experience can save both time and expense of travelling to the shops and being online, there are no restrictions on trading hours.

Good choice of replacement rugby boots on offer, covering both juniors and adults. A simple registration process is required to realise the full Re-Boot benefits.

Recyling of unwanted rugby boots also helps contribute to an improved carbon footprint through reducing the quantity of material that enters landfill waste.
